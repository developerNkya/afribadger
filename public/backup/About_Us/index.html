Welcome to Camel Leon Safaris Ltd., where adventure meets adaptation. Inspired by the chameleon’s ability to thrive in diverse environments, our founders created Camel Leon Safaris to share the unparalleled beauty of Tanzania’s wild landscapes with explorers from around the world. Our journey began with a deep love for nature and a commitment to showcasing Tanzania’s rich biodiversity. We specialise in crafting bespoke safari experiences that connect you with the heart of nature. From thrilling wildlife encounters to immersive cultural tours, our adventures are designed to inspire and rejuvenate

Our dedicated travel planners work tirelessly to create personalized itineraries tailored to your preferences. They meticulously plan every detail, from accommodations to activities, ensuring a seamless and memorable experience. Their commitment to excellence ensures that your safari is uniquely yours.

Our sustainability experts focus on preserving the natural beauty of Tanzania. They implement eco-friendly practices in our operations and work with local communities to promote conservation and sustainable tourism. Their efforts help us protect the environment and support local livelihoods.

Our expert safari guides are the cornerstone of our exceptional service. With extensive knowledge of Tanzania’s wildlife, landscapes, and local culture, they provide enriching insights and ensure your safety and enjoyment throughout the journey. Their passion for nature and storytelling will make your safari both educational and thrilling.

Our customer support team is always ready to assist you with any inquiries or needs you may have. They are dedicated to providing prompt, friendly, and efficient service, making sure your experience with Camel Leon Safaris is smooth from start to finish.

Behind every great safari is a skilled logistics team ensuring everything runs smoothly. From transportation to accommodations, they handle all the behind-the-scenes details, allowing you to focus on enjoying your adventure.

Together, our team at Camel Leon Safaris Ltd. is committed to providing you with an extraordinary safari experience, filled with awe-inspiring moments and lifelong memories. Join us and discover the wonders of Tanzania with a team that truly cares.

At Camel Leon Safaris Ltd., our team is the heart of our company, each member bringing their passion, expertise, and dedication to ensure you have an unforgettable adventure.

At Camel Leon Safaris, we prioritise creating exceptional, personalised experiences for our clients. Our expert guides have extensive knowledge of local culture and attractions, ensuring every safari is enriching and unique. With a focus on sustainable tourism practices, we aim to preserve the natural beauty of Tanzania for future generations.

Founders

Our founders, inspired by the chameleon’s adaptability, envisioned creating safari experiences that allow travelers to immerse themselves in the beauty of Tanzania. Their vision and commitment to sustainable tourism have shaped Camel Leon Safaris into what it is today.
